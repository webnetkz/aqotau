Brand New Furniture & Rental Appliances

At rent4keeps we know the importance of value for money. That’s why we offer the latest in an extensive range of technology, appliances and furniture rentals and more, all on great value rental plans.
Our product range includes:

  • Mobile Phones
  • Computers & Tablets
  • TV & Audio
  • Home furniture
  • Kitchen Appliances
  • Laundry Appliances
  • And more…

To rent any of our new appliances or furniture, you simply choose the product and then one of our friendly representatives visits you to arrange the rental agreement. Before you know it your new rental product is delivered and ready for use, easy as 1...2...3!

 

No Upfront Payments

We understand that cash isn’t always available, especially when you need something right away.

This is why at rent4keeps we offer flexible rental agreements with no upfront payments, this allows our customers to get their new products quickly. Our rental plans are made to suit your circumstances, allowing you to pay for your product in affordable amounts.

 

Warranty on All Products

To protect you against any faulty items, we ensure that all of our products carry full manufacturer and extended warranties.

This way, you can rest assure that when you rent furniture & appliances at rent4keeps, you’re getting a high quality product that you will be able to use throughout the duration of your rental agreement.

 

Rent To Buy With Poor Credit

Rent4keeps understand that some customers might not have the best credit history, so our team will work with you to provide a flexible rental term to suit your needs. Our plans provide great value and cater to all your household needs.

Whatever products you choose to rent our professional local team will create an agreement to suit your specific circumstances. We can also arrange installation, so there’s less hassle for you.

 

Great Service & Delivery on All Products

Our professional representatives pride themselves on providing the best customer service in the market. Once you contact us, we come to you quickly. Our friendly representatives are experienced and very helpful in providing you with an affordable rental plan.

Our aim is to ensure that our customers receive the best service, as soon as you sign up for your brand new product, we get it to you quickly! With our delivery included, you can expect fast delivery on all your furniture and appliance rental products.
