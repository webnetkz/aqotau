Product Description

The popular all terrain buggy, that is fully featured in a good comfortable size, includes a superior one had fast fold, a conveniently located hand braking system, rear wheel suspension, 12″ airfilled tyres and stylish canvas colours.

A versatile all rounder, that is perfect for in the city or off the beaten track – great for families who desire flexibility on an award winning platform.
